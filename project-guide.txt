### 2. MOCKUPS: HOW TO HANDLE LIVE LINKS WITHOUT MOCKUPS?

Since you have live links but no professional mockups (images), follow these steps to make your portfolio look premium:

#### Step 1: Use Screenshot Framing Tools (Recommended)
Don't just use raw screenshots. Use these free tools to "frame" your live websites/apps:
- **Shot.so**: My #1 recommendation. Upload a screenshot of your live link, and it puts it in a beautiful browser or phone frame with a glassmorphism background.
- **ScreenShot.rocks**: Great for quick browser/mobile frames.
- **Previewed.app**: If you want 3D mobile mockups for your React Native apps.

#### Step 2: Record "Live" GIFs/Videos
For your mobile apps (Expo/React Native), record a 10-second video of you navigating the app.
- Use **Loom** or **Screenity** to record.
- Convert to `.mp4` or `.gif`.
- Place these in the `Work` section instead of static images. It looks much more professional than a mockup!
